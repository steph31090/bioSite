About

Jesus Lara was born in Nayarit, Mexico, and raised in La Habra, California, where his upbringing gave him a strong sense of family and responsibility. From a young age, he dreamed of becoming a police officer, a goal that reflected his desire to serve and protect others. Along the way, he was shaped by several important figures in his life. His father instilled in him the values of hard work and caring for family, while his high school teacher, Dr. Hawkins, encouraged him to stay focused and determined in everything he pursued.

Today, Jesus balances a busy professional life with a variety of personal interests. He works as a project engineer for a construction company and serves as a part-time manager at a Mexican restaurant. His current career goal is to move into a project manager position, leading his own projects and guiding a team. Outside of work, he has a personal goal of buying a home soon, something he is committed to achieving for himself and his family.

In his free time, Jesus enjoys going to the gym several times a week, a habit that began after a back injury and evolved into a passion for health and strength. He also enjoys reading to expand his vocabulary and knowledge, and he has a long-standing love for building LEGO sets, especially Star Wars-themed models, a hobby that began with childhood birthday gifts from his aunt.

Of all his accomplishments, Jesus is most proud of his marriage. For 13 years, he and his wife have built a strong life together, proving doubters wrong and surpassing the odds often associated with young marriages. Those who know him best describe Jesus as hardworking, family-oriented, and driven, qualities that continue to guide his life and goals.
